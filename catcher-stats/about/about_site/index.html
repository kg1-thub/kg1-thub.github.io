    Catcher/Score

    This site contains records regarding wins, losses, saves, and runs scored by pitchers by catcher for each NPB team.

    In MLB, pitching defensive stats by catcher are compiled into a score called cERA (Catcher's ERA). The cERA is posted on the website of ESPN, an American sports channel, from 2002 to 2017.>ESPN(MLB Player Fielding Stats - As C)

    SHO-METER

    From 2024 onward, Shohei Ohtani's success in the games he plays in is visualized in a chart called a heat map. The graph also displays Shohei Ohtani's season record, and graphs of his hits, home runs, stolen bases, and runs batted in.
